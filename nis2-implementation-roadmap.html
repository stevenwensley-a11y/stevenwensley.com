Key Programme Risks

HIGH

Multi-Jurisdiction Complexity

3 EU member states + Norway (EEA). Different transposition timelines and sector-specific authorities.

Mitigation: Unified compliance framework with country-specific addenda
HIGH

OT/IT Convergence

Production SCADA systems may lack modern security controls. Segmentation without disrupting operations.

Mitigation: Phased OT assessment with production-safe testing windows
MEDIUM

Supply Chain Depth

Global supply chain with varying digital maturity. Tier-2+ supplier visibility limited.

Mitigation: Risk-based tiering, focus SAQs on critical suppliers first
MEDIUM

Swedish Transposition Delay

Sweden (HQ jurisdiction) still in legislative process. Requirements may shift.

Mitigation: Build to directive baseline, adapt when national law finalised
MEDIUM

HQ/Group Alignment

Swiss parent may have own security standards. Need to align Nordic programme with group framework.

Mitigation: Early engagement with HQ CISO/security team
MEDIUM

Resource Availability

Nordic team of ~130. Key SMEs may be stretched across compliance + BAU operations.

Mitigation: Dedicated programme resource + clear time allocation agreements
