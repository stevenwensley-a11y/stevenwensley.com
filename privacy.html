Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data on stevenwensley.com.

1. About this website

This privacy policy applies to stevenwensley.com, a professional services website operated by Steven Seidenfaden Wensley, based in Copenhagen, Denmark.

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages and uses Formspree for form submissions and lead capture. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect and use data.

2. Data we collect

We collect data in two ways:

A. Form submissions (Formspree)

When you submit a form on this website—such as booking a session, downloading AI Governance templates, or requesting information—we collect the following personal data:

  • Email address
  • Name
  • Organization / company
  • Job title / role
  • Industry
  • Organization size
  • Any additional information you provide in message fields

Form submission is optional. You are not required to submit any forms to use the free resources on this site (such as the AI Governance Assessment tool).

B. Technical data

This website does not set cookies or collect tracking data on its own. We do not use Google Analytics or other third-party analytics services at this time.

Your browser may automatically send technical information (IP address, browser type, page visited, referrer) to GitHub Pages as part of standard web server logging. This is not under our direct control and is governed by GitHub's privacy policy.

3. How we use your data

We use the data you provide through forms for these purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiry or request for information
  • To send you the materials you requested (e.g., templates, assessment PDFs)
  • To arrange and conduct the service you've requested (e.g., a consultation or strategy session)
  • To follow up after a service engagement with relevant updates or offers
  • To understand which services are most relevant to our audience (aggregated insights)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is used only to deliver the services you've requested and to improve our offerings.

4. Data storage and security

Form submissions are stored by Formspree, a US-based form processing service. Formspree stores your data on their servers and is responsible for its security.

Formspree's privacy practices: For details on how Formspree collects, stores, and protects data, please refer to their Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

We recommend you review Formspree's privacy policy to understand how they handle your information. If you have concerns about Formspree's data practices, you may contact them directly at privacy@formspree.io.

5. GDPR and your rights

If you are located in the European Union or UK, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). These include:

  • Right of access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can correct inaccurate data or complete incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure: You can request deletion of your data in certain circumstances (the "right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restrict processing: You can request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability: You can request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to object: You can object to processing of your data for direct marketing.
  • Right to withdraw consent: If we process data based on your consent, you can withdraw it at any time.
