Step 3

AI Literacy Workshop

DKK 15,000-25,000
1 day · 8-20 participants

EU AI Act Article 4 has required AI literacy for all organisations deploying or using AI systems since February 2025. This workshop delivers role-based, hands-on training with your own AI systems and industry-specific cases. Each participant receives a personal competency certificate as Article 4 compliance documentation.

What's included

  • Pre-workshop needs analysis and customisation
  • Role-based teaching: decision-makers, implementers, users
  • Industry-specific cases and ethical dilemmas
  • AI risk matrix exercise with your own systems
  • EU AI Act and NIS2 regulatory overview
  • Materials and slides for internal knowledge sharing
  • 30-day follow-up Q&A session (1 hour, virtual)

Deliverables

  • Personal competency certificate per participant (Article 4)
  • Organisation AI Literacy plan with competency levels
  • Industry-tailored case collection for internal training
  • Completed AI risk matrix for your systems

Step 4

ISO 42001 Gap Analysis

DKK 45,000-75,000
2-3 weeks

ISO 42001 is the international standard for AI Management Systems. This structured gap analysis maps your current maturity against all standard requirements, identifies gaps, and delivers an executive-ready report with a prioritised certification roadmap and business case.

What's included

  • Kick-off workshop with project team
  • 6-10 interviews with key stakeholders
  • Document review of existing policies and processes
  • Full mapping against ISO 42001 Annex A controls
  • Gap scoring on 4 maturity levels per control
  • Risk prioritisation of identified gaps
  • Executive presentation of findings

Deliverables

  • Gap analysis report (30-50 pages)
  • Maturity scorecard with heatmap
  • Prioritised roadmap to certification (6-18 months)
  • Business case with ROI calculation
  • Executive summary (2 pages) for leadership/board
  • Risk register with top 10 governance risks

Governance Implementation

DKK 150,000-350,000
3-6 months

What's included
